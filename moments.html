Moments

Finding a few moments each day to take a few deep breaths and quiet your mind is a great way to relieve stress and improve your overall health. The Moments app and necklace work together to help you decompress and stay centred throughout your busy day by tracking your breathing, analyzing your heart rate, and guiding you through moments of complete mindfulness.
